Cicada Creative Magazine is an all-arts interdisciplinary magazine for undergraduate students.

We were first founded in 2018, the product of seven undergraduate students at Colorado State University meeting at a coffee shop on the corner of campus. We wanted to assemble something we’d never seen before: a creative magazine accessible to all undergraduate students that reached beyond conventional creative constraints. From its inception, Cicada was meant to offer a new, never-before seen platform, one that didn’t define itself by rigid definitions of art or writing.

We’ve evolved a lot since then, at times stumbling on our path, and at others, methodically clearing it. There are things we’ve always worked toward: creating community, capturing authenticity, pushing boundaries – and it has helped us grow. Since our beginnings at CSU, we’ve branched out. We have editors and reviewers from across the United States, and we accept submissions in any genre from any undergraduate student, wherever they might be.

In pursuit of our values, we’ve done away with the traditional submission limits of “art” and “writing.” Instead, we happily accept any creative work from any discipline. Since that’s an incredibly broad statement, we’ve broken down our submission categories into five alternative types: auditory, spatial, language-based, visual, and cultural/social. This includes fiction, nonfiction, poetry, and art – but it also includes research, creative data, architecture, culinary art, cosmetology, and feature writing, to name a few.

While we will gladly review any and all submissions from any and all undergraduate students, the work we ultimately publish is driven by experiment, passion, and hard work. If it pushes the boundaries of convention, shape, and form, we’d love to see it. We want your hybrid creation, your brainchild, your magnum opus. We acknowledge that heart-driven creativity often does not come box-shaped, perfectly ready to fall into a category. Cicada Creative Magazine exists to support your quirks, your nuances, your isms – your identity.

We hope to broadcast each and every way you make your mark on our world. You will always be able to reach us, either in-person, through our website, on Instagram, or by email at info@cicadacreativemag.com. We’ll see you soon.

Stay creative,
-CCM
