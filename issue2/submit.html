Submissions are currently open.

Hi again, everyone. We're currently accepting submissions for Issue 03 of Cicada Creative Magazine. Submissions will close on March 23. Our theme this semester is identity.

Identity is the complicated aggregation of all the things that make you--well, you. Our experiences, the people around us, and the thousands of defining moments we've carried with us shape who we are and how we engage with the world. This semester, we're calling for work that explores identity and answers the question, "who are you?" In a time our on-campus identity has become increasingly fragmented by implicit bias and intolerance, we want to amplify the voices that get talked over, the voices that are treated as less important. We're specifically asking for more nontraditional forms of art this semester--from a line of code to a yarn sculpture, we're here for it.
